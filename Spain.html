My Travels To Spain!

These are some of the cities I visited on my trip:

  • Madrid
  • Barcelona
  • Marbella, Malaga

My Experiences

I was lucky enough to be able to take my parents to Spain to celebrate my mom's birthday! I was absolutely enamoured by the country, especially Barcelona. I saw beautiful beaches, incredible architecture, and experienced a new culture. I would definitely visit again!

Attractions

There are endless beautiful places you can visit while in Spain. My top three recommendations would be:

  1. La Sagrada Familia Basilica
  2. L'Arc de Triomf
  3. Mercado de la Boqueria

Travel Tips

When traveling in Spain or across Europe, I would recommend a few things. First, learn a bit of Spanish if you can and if you don't already know. Next, keep your belongings safe on you. A crossbody bag is recommended. Also, better to leave any valuables behind. Finally, if you are taking public transportation across the country, such as the rail system, including high-speed trains (AVE), be wary of scams or fake sites. I found it easier to purchase on-site and in-person. Here is a link to one of their official websites for one of the more economical options: Renfe | Train Tickets AVE
