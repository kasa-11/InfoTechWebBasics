Travel Tips

When traveling in Sweden or across Europe, I would recommend a few things. Of course, try to learn a bit of Swedish if you can. Although, much of the country speaks English as well. Luckily I had my aunt to help translate for us. Next, become familiar with the public transportation. This is how I got around everyday and while it wasn't the most complicated to use, there were some stops in the "Tunnelbana" the Swedish subway, that was quite busy. Generally when traveling, try to keep your belongings safe on you. A crossbody bag is recommended.
